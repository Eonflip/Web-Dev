Veggie Stew

Description

This recipe assumes that you have access to an instant pot, however if you do not a large pot should suffice but cook times may increase.

This is a recipe for a delicious veggie stew! If you substitute the butter for oil or a non dairy butter it can also be vegan. It should have all the flavor and taste you'd need in a dish

I like to cook this on a cold night and warm my tummy, its easy and should only take you about 30 minutes total including prepping the ingredients. This recipe can feed 4-6 (depending on your appetite haha my partner and I usually eat this for a few meals a week when we're feeling lazy but still want a nutritious meal

Ingredients List

  • 2 Cups Veggie Broth
  • 2-3 Cups of Mushrooms (White, Cremini, or Baby Bella)
  • 2 Tbsp Butter or Olive Oil (I like to use both)
  • 3-4 Large Carrots
  • 2 Cups Spinach or Kale
  • 3-4 Cups Chopped Red or Yellow Potatoes (I like to use both)
  • 4-6 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • (Optional) 2 Turnips
  • Salt - To taste
  • Pepper - To taste
  • Paprika - To taste

Steps:

  1. Chop the garlic, onion and potatos
  2. Add the butter, oil or both into the instant pot
  3. Add the chopped garlic, onion and potatos
  4. Set the pot to sauté and stir ingredients while sautéing until onions turn translucent
  5. Once ingredients have sautéd add in veggie broth and chopped carrots, mushrooms, and turnips(if using)
  6. Put the lid on and set the instant pot to pressure cook on high for 9 minutes
  7. Once finished release pressure on instant pot and remove lid
  8. Add in the spinach or kale and season with salt, pepper and paprika (if you prefer different spices feel free to experiment with what tastes best to you!
  9. Cover once more with the lid and set to pressure cook another 3-4 minutes depending on how wilted you like your greens
  10. Once finished release pressure on instant pot serve and enjoy!
